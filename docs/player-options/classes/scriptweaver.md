# Scriptweaver
*Magic is an ancient language. You just speak it in code.*

---

## Class Overview
Scriptweavers blend arcane principles with advanced programming, rewriting the digital and metaphysical reality around them. They wield "script magic" — a hybrid of spells, hacks, and algorithms — to disable enemies, shield allies, and manipulate the battlefield.

---

## Class Features

**Hit Dice:** 1d6 per Scriptweaver level  
**Hit Points at 1st Level:** 6 + your Constitution modifier  
**Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d6 (or 4) + your Constitution modifier per Scriptweaver level after 1st  

**Armor Proficiencies:** Light armor  
**Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple weapons  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Programmer’s kit (specialized hacking tools)  
**Saving Throws:** Intelligence, Wisdom  
**Skills:** Choose two from Arcana, Insight, Investigation, Perception, Persuasion, Technology  

---

### Starting Equipment
You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:  
- (a) simple weapon or (b) light pistol  
- (a) light armor or (b) armored coat  
- Programmer’s kit  
- 30 credits  

---

## Script Points (SP)
Script Points power the Scriptweaver’s abilities, functioning similarly to spell slots but with more flexibility.  
- **Starts at 4 at level 1**, scaling to 30 by level 20.  
- **Regains on short or long rest**.  
- Can be spent on **programs** (your equivalent of spells), cyberhacks, or subclass features.

## Class Abilities

### Programcasting
At 1st level, you gain the ability to execute **programs** — effects that combine elements of magic and hacking.  
- You know a number of programs equal to your Intelligence modifier + Scriptweaver level.  
- You can write new programs into your digital grimoire after finding them in data vaults or from other Scriptweavers.  
- Casting time, range, and effects follow program descriptions.  

---

### Hack Boost
Also at 1st level, spend 1 SP to gain advantage on any Technology or Arcana check for 1 minute.

---

### Digital Shield
At 2nd level, as a reaction, spend 2 SP to give yourself or an ally +2 AC until the start of your next turn.

---

### Scriptweaver Path
At 3rd level, choose your coding discipline:  
- **Path of the Glitch Mage** – Battlefield control through reality distortion.  
- **Path of the Cyber Mystic** – Buffs, debuffs, and support programs.  
- **Path of the Quantum Hacker** – Direct system manipulation and sabotage.

---

## Scriptweaver Paths

### Path of the Glitch Mage
*Reality is just another program to debug.*  
- **3rd Level – Warp Field:** Spend 2 SP to bend space in a 10-ft. radius, making the area difficult terrain for enemies.  
- **6th Level – Visual Corruption:** Spend 3 SP to impose disadvantage on attack rolls against you until your next turn.  
- **10th Level – Phase Desync:** Spend 4 SP to teleport up to 60 ft. as an action.  
- **14th Level – Collapse Kernel:** Spend 6 SP to force all enemies in a 20-ft. radius to make a Dex save or take 6d8 force damage.  
- **18th Level – Reality Flicker:** Once per long rest, become incorporeal for 1 minute.

---

### Path of the Cyber Mystic
*Wisdom in the code, strength in the system.*  
- **3rd Level – Protocol Blessing:** Spend 2 SP to give an ally +1d4 to all rolls for 1 minute.  
- **6th Level – Code Purge:** Spend 3 SP to remove one condition from an ally.  
- **10th Level – Firewall Ward:** Spend 4 SP to give an ally resistance to all damage for 1 round.  
- **14th Level – System Restore:** Spend 6 SP to heal an ally for 6d8 + your Intelligence modifier.  
- **18th Level – Master Protocol:** Once per long rest, cast *mass heal* without components.

---

### Path of the Quantum Hacker
*Every lock has a backdoor — even the universe.*  
- **3rd Level – Data Spike:** Spend 2 SP to deal 2d8 lightning damage to a target within 60 ft. and disable one device it’s using.  
- **6th Level – Remote Override:** Spend 3 SP to control a mechanical or electronic device within 60 ft. for 1 minute.  
- **10th Level – Chain Hack:** Spend 4 SP to disable up to three devices or constructs within 30 ft.  
- **14th Level – System Crash:** Spend 6 SP to deal 8d8 lightning damage to a machine or construct.  
- **18th Level – Digital Dominion:** Once per long rest, take control of all machines within 120 ft. for 1 minute.

---

### Overclock Program
At 5th level, spend 2 SP to cast a program as a bonus action if it normally takes an action.

---

### System Scramble
At 7th level, spend 3 SP to impose disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks for one creature until the start of your next turn.

---

### Data Surge
At 9th level, when you roll initiative, regain 1d4 SP.

---

### Optimized Code
At 11th level, reduce the SP cost of one program you cast each turn by 1 (minimum 1).

---

### Firewall Override
At 13th level, spend 5 SP to make an area immune to teleportation, planar travel, and remote hacking for 1 minute.

---

### Code Mastery
At 15th level, you can maintain concentration on two programs at once, but both require concentration checks if you take damage.

---

### Ghost Code
At 17th level, once per short or long rest, you can hide yourself from all sensors and magical detection for 1 minute.

---

### Reality Rewrite
At 20th level, once per long rest, spend 10 SP to cast *wish* without material components, representing the ultimate script execution.

---

## Multiclassing
