# Warjack
*Metal is stronger than flesh — and you are proof.*

---

## Class Overview
Warjacks are cybernetic soldiers built for frontline combat. They leverage cutting-edge augments and brute force to dominate the battlefield, shrugging off damage that would fell lesser beings and delivering devastating counterattacks.

---

## Class Features

**Hit Dice:** 1d12 per Warjack level  
**Hit Points at 1st Level:** 12 + your Constitution modifier  
**Hit Points at Higher Levels:** 1d12 (or 7) + your Constitution modifier per Warjack level after 1st  

**Armor Proficiencies:** Light armor, medium armor, heavy armor, shields  
**Weapon Proficiencies:** Simple and martial weapons  
**Tool Proficiencies:** None  
**Saving Throws:** Strength, Constitution  
**Skills:** Choose two from Athletics, Intimidation, Perception, Survival, Technology  

---

### Starting Equipment
You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:  
- (a) martial weapon and shield or (b) two martial weapons  
- (a) chainmail or (b) combat armor  
- 50 credits  

---

## Overdrive Points (ODP)
Overdrive Points fuel the Warjack’s combat systems and cybernetic boosts.  
- **Starts at 2 at level 1**, scaling to 20 by level 20.  
- **Regains on short or long rest**.  
- Spent to activate augments, temporary boosts, and path features.

## Class Abilities

### Cybernetic Frame
At 1st level, your body is reinforced with cybernetics. Your base AC is 13 + Dexterity modifier when not wearing armor, and you have advantage on saves against being stunned.

---

### Overdrive
At 1st level, you can spend 1 ODP to gain +2 to attack rolls and +2d6 damage on melee attacks until the end of your turn.

---

### Combat Surge
At 2nd level, spend 1 ODP as a bonus action to gain +10 ft. movement and advantage on your next attack.

---

### Warjack Path
At 3rd level, choose your cybernetic specialization:  
- **Path of the Ironclad** – Extreme defense and damage mitigation.  
- **Path of the Siegebreaker** – Heavy weapon master and demolitions expert.  
- **Path of the Berserker Core** – Overdrive-fueled close-range destruction.

---

## Warjack Paths

### Path of the Ironclad
*The wall that will not fall.*  
- **3rd Level – Reinforced Plating:** Gain +1 AC while not wearing armor.  
- **6th Level – Bulwark Protocol:** Spend 2 ODP to gain resistance to all damage until the start of your next turn.  
- **10th Level – Shielded Advance:** Allies within 10 ft. gain +2 AC while you’re conscious.  
- **14th Level – Indomitable Core:** Once per long rest, drop to 1 HP instead of 0.  
- **18th Level – Fortress Mode:** Spend 4 ODP to become immune to forced movement and gain advantage on all saving throws for 1 minute.

---

### Path of the Siegebreaker
*Nothing stands in your way.*  
- **3rd Level – Breach Specialist:** Deal double damage to objects and structures.  
- **6th Level – Demolition Charge:** Spend 2 ODP to make a melee attack that deals an additional 3d8 thunder damage to creatures and double to structures.  
- **10th Level – Heavy Ordinance:** You can use heavy weapons without penalty, and they deal +1d8 damage.  
- **14th Level – Explosive Rampage:** Once per turn, deal an extra 1d6 damage to all creatures within 5 ft. after hitting with a melee attack.  
- **18th Level – Annihilator Strike:** Once per long rest, spend 5 ODP to make a single melee attack dealing triple damage.

---

### Path of the Berserker Core
*Your systems were not designed to last — they were designed to win.*  
- **3rd Level – Frenzy Drive:** Spend 1 ODP to gain +10 ft. movement and +1 attack until the end of your turn.  
- **6th Level – Overheat:** When you drop a creature to 0 HP, regain 1 ODP.  
- **10th Level – Berserker Shield:** Gain temporary hit points equal to your Warjack level when you enter Overdrive.  
- **14th Level – Kill Protocol:** Spend 3 ODP to make an additional attack whenever you take the Attack action.  
- **18th Level – Meltdown Mode:** Once per long rest, enter a berserk state for 1 minute: double your Strength score (max 30), +2 AC, and melee attacks deal an additional 2d8 damage.

---

### Extra Attack
At 5th level, you can attack twice whenever you take the Attack action.

---

### Shockwave Slam
At 7th level, spend 2 ODP to smash the ground or a target, dealing 2d6 thunder damage to all creatures within 10 ft. and forcing a Strength save or be knocked prone.

---

### Juggernaut Protocol
At 9th level, you can move through enemy spaces without penalty and have advantage on checks to shove or grapple.

---

### Overclock
At 11th level, you may roll an additional damage die when using Overdrive.

---

### Hardened Systems
At 13th level, you gain resistance to critical hits; when crit, roll damage normally.

---

### Titan Mode
At 15th level, once per long rest, gain +2 AC and resistance to all damage for 1 minute.

---

### Unstoppable Force
At 17th level, you can push enemies up to 15 ft. away when you hit them with a melee attack.

---

### Apex Warjack
At 20th level, your Strength and Constitution scores each increase by 2, and their maximums increase to 24.

---

## Multiclassing
**Prerequisite:** Strength 15, Constitution 15  
**Proficiencies Gained:** Medium armor, heavy armor, shields, martial weapons
