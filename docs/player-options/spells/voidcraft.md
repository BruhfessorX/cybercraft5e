# Voidcraft Spells

## Whispering Circuit
*1st-level Enchantment, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 60 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You implant an eldritch "bug" into a target’s mind or machine. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw or be plagued by maddening whispers. While affected, they have disadvantage on attack rolls and concentration checks. The target may repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns.

---

## Spatial Fracture
*4th-level Conjuration, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 60 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Instantaneous  

You rend open a tear in space at a point you can see. Each creature within 15 feet must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 4d10 force damage and is teleported 30 feet in a random direction.

---

## Nulllight
*2nd-level Evocation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 30 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You create an aura of pure void that devours light and sound. Creatures within 15 feet are effectively blinded and deafened unless they have void or psychic senses.

---

## Abyssal Grasp
*3rd-level Conjuration, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 20 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

Shadowy tendrils erupt from the ground, restraining up to two creatures of your choice. They must succeed on a Strength saving throw or take 3d6 necrotic damage at the start of their turn while restrained.

---

## Veilwalk
*5th-level Transmutation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action  
- **Range:** Self  
- **Components:** V  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You phase partially into the Void, becoming incorporeal. You can move through objects and creatures as difficult terrain, have resistance to all damage except force, and may teleport up to 60 feet as a bonus action.

---

### Umbral Flicker
*Cantrip, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action  
- **Range:** 15 feet  
- **Components:** V  
- **Duration:** Instantaneous  

You instantly teleport to an unoccupied space within range that is in dim light or darkness.

---

### Veil Leak
*2nd-level Conjuration, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 30 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes  

You tear a small rift between realities, creating a 10-foot radius zone of spatial distortion. Attacks into or out of the zone are made with disadvantage.

---

### Hollow Lance
*4th-level Necromancy, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 60 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Instantaneous  

You conjure a spear of pure void energy. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw, taking 6d10 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half on a success. On a failed save, they are also **frightened** until the start of your next turn.

---

### Oblivion Step
*5th-level Transmutation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action  
- **Range:** Self  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You partially shift yourself into the void, becoming incorporeal. You can move through creatures and solid objects, but take 1d10 force damage if you end your turn inside an object. You cannot physically interact with the material world except through spells.

---

### Event Horizon
*7th-level Evocation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 60 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You create a gravitational singularity in a 20-foot radius sphere. Each creature in the area takes 5d10 force damage and is pulled 10 feet toward the center at the start of its turn. The area is difficult terrain.  

---

### Null Pulse  
*Cantrip, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 15 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Instantaneous  

You emit a concussive wave of void energy. A creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d8 force damage and lose concentration if maintaining a spell or digital program.  

---

### Entropic Lash  
*1st-level Evocation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 30 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

A black tendril of decaying energy lashes a creature, dealing 2d8 necrotic damage. On a failed Constitution save, its armor or cybernetics corrode slightly—reducing AC by 1 until repaired or the spell ends.

---

### Fractured Veil  
*2nd-level Illusion, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 30 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** 1 minute  

You fracture visible space into mirrored shards. Choose a 10-foot cube. Any ranged attack targeting a creature inside the area has a 50% chance to hit an illusion instead. When the spell ends, the shards collapse with a faint static pop.

---

### Temporal Slip  
*3rd-level Transmutation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action  
- **Range:** Self  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You step a heartbeat out of sync with reality. Until the spell ends, you can take one extra reaction each round and attacks against you are made with disadvantage. Each time you end a turn while phased, take 1d4 force damage.

---

### Hollow Gate  
*3rd-level Conjuration, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute  
- **Range:** 10 feet  
- **Components:** V, S, M (a shard of void crystal worth 50 credits)  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour  

You open a thin mirror-black portal linking two points within 1 mile. The gate allows instant travel for creatures that step through, but each passage costs them 1 temporary sanity damage due to spatial disorientation.

---

### Abyssal Mirror  
*4th-level Enchantment, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 60 feet  
- **Components:** V, S, M (a reflective surface)  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

Target one creature. An inverted reflection of them crawls from the mirror’s surface, mimicking their movements with hostility. The duplicate uses the target’s statistics but has 1 hit point and acts immediately after them. When slain, it collapses into dark static and the caster gains 2 corruption points.

---

### Null Horizon  
*5th-level Evocation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 60 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You erase light and sound in a 20-foot radius sphere. All creatures inside are blinded and deafened, and no sound passes through the boundary. Magic or tech relying on sensory data (like targeting systems) automatically fails while within.  

---

### Void Echo  
*5th-level Necromancy, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 minute  
- **Range:** Touch  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** 10 minutes  

You call forth the shade of a recently dead creature. You can ask up to three questions before it dissolves. Each question costs you 1 sanity damage. The echo cannot lie but may answer cryptically, filtered through fragmented perception.  

---

### Event Fracture  
*6th-level Transmutation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 90 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

Reality bends around a chosen 30-foot area. At the start of each round, roll a d6:  
1-2: Gravity reverses.  
3-4: Time slows; creatures move at half speed.  
5-6: Space folds; any ranged attack entering the area redirects to a random edge.  
Creatures in the zone take 5d8 force damage the first time they enter or start their turn inside each round.

---

### Collapse Singularity  
*7th-level Evocation, Voidcraft*  
- **Casting Time:** 1 action  
- **Range:** 100 feet  
- **Components:** V, S  
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute  

You collapse a fragment of the Veil into a tiny singularity. Each creature in a 30-foot radius must make a Strength saving throw or take 10d8 force damage. Those reduced to 0 HP are folded into nonexistence—leaving behind warped shadows where they stood.
