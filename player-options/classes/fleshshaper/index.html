    Fleshshaper¶

    You are your own experiment — and the battlefield is your lab.

    Class Overview¶

    Fleshshapers wield biotech and body modification to reshape themselves and others. They can grow natural weapons, mutate their physiology, and unleash organic horrors. Their craft blurs the line between healer, warrior, and monstrosity.

    Class Features¶

    Hit Dice: 1d8 per Fleshshaper level
    Hit Points at 1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
    Hit Points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Fleshshaper level after 1st

    Armor Proficiencies: Light armor, Medium armor
    Weapon Proficiencies: Simple weapons, Natural weapons (special)
    Tool Proficiencies: Biotech kit
    Saving Throws: Constitution, Wisdom
    Skills: Choose two from Animal Handling, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Nature, Survival, Technology

    Starting Equipment¶

    You start with the following equipment, in addition to the equipment granted by your background:
    - (a) simple weapon or (b) biotech scalpel (counts as a dagger)
    - (a) light armor or (b) medium armor
    - Biotech kit
    - 30 credits

    Mutation Points (MP)¶

    Mutation Points fuel the Fleshshaper’s abilities.
    - Starts at 3 at level 1, scaling to 25 by level 20.
    - Regains on short or long rest.
    - Can be used to grow temporary mutations, heal, or unleash bio-attacks.

    Class Abilities¶

    Bioform¶

    At 1st level, your body is partially modified. Choose one:
    - Claws: 1d6 slashing natural weapon.
    - Spines: Ranged natural weapon, 1d6 piercing damage, 20/60 ft.
    - Fangs: 1d6 piercing damage, lifesteal equal to Constitution modifier.

    Mutate¶

    At 1st level, as a bonus action, spend 1 MP to grow a minor mutation for 1 minute (claws, extra eyes, muscle growth, etc.). Only one minor mutation can be active at a time.

    Regenerative Tissues¶

    At 2nd level, as a bonus action, spend 2 MP to regain hit points equal to 1d8 + Constitution modifier.

    Fleshshaper Path¶

    At 3rd level, choose your specialization:
    - Path of the Biomancer – Healing and support mutations.
    - Path of the Predator – Combat-focused natural weapons.
    - Path of the Aberrant – Monstrous mutations and battlefield control.

    Fleshshaper Paths¶

    Path of the Biomancer¶

    Your body heals and others follow.
    - 3rd Level – Bioheal: Spend 2 MP to heal an ally 1d8 + Constitution modifier.
    - 6th Level – Cleansing Enzymes: Spend 3 MP to remove a condition from a creature.
    - 10th Level – Growth Bloom: Spend 4 MP to grant an ally +2 AC for 1 minute via symbiotic armor.
    - 14th Level – Cellular Renewal: Heal an ally 4d8 + Con mod as an action.
    - 18th Level – Master Healer: All healing you provide is maximized.

    Path of the Predator¶

    Evolve to hunt.
    - 3rd Level – Bestial Fury: Spend 1 MP to add 1d6 to your next natural weapon attack.
    - 6th Level – Pounce: Spend 2 MP to leap up to 20 ft. and make a melee attack as a bonus action.
    - 10th Level – Rend: If you hit the same target twice in one turn with natural weapons, deal an extra 1d8 damage.
    - 14th Level – Savage Evolution: Natural weapon damage increases by one die step.
    - 18th Level – Apex Predator: Advantage on all melee attacks for 1 minute once per long rest.

    Path of the Aberrant¶

    You are the nightmare in the flesh.
    - 3rd Level – Tentacle Whip: Spend 1 MP to make a melee attack with 10 ft. reach.
    - 6th Level – Spore Cloud: Spend 2 MP to create a 10-ft. radius cloud; enemies must make a Con save or be poisoned.
    - 10th Level – Mass Mutation: You can maintain two minor mutations at once.
    - 14th Level – Horror Form: Spend 5 MP to become Large and gain +2 AC for 1 minute.
    - 18th Level – True Aberration: Once per long rest, transform into a terrifying form for 1 minute: +2 AC, advantage on all attacks, resistance to all damage.

    Multi-Mutation¶

    At 5th level, maintain two different mutations at once.

    Aberrant Step¶

    At 7th level, gain climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

    Chitinous Plating¶

    At 9th level, your AC increases by +1.

    Rapid Mutation¶

    At 11th level, growing a mutation no longer costs an action; it becomes a bonus action.

    Adaptive Resistance¶

    At 13th level, when you take damage, spend 3 MP to gain resistance to that damage type until the end of your next turn.

    Monstrous Form¶

    At 15th level, once per long rest, transform into a Large mutant for 1 minute: +2 AC, +2 to attack rolls, double reach.

    Aberrant Mastery¶

    At 17th level, you can maintain three different mutations at once.

    Apex Mutation¶

    At 20th level, you permanently gain one major mutation of your choice (GM approval).

    Multiclassing¶

    Prerequisite: Constitution 13, Wisdom 13
    Proficiencies Gained: Light armor, medium armor, biotech kit
