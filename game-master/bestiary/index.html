    Bestiary¶

    Sample enemies for Cybercraft campaigns.

    Veilspawn¶

    Twisted creatures from beyond reality.

    Techno-Liches¶

    Hackers who uploaded themselves into cursed networks.

    Shadow Constructs¶

    Animated machines powered by eldritch cores.

    Full stat blocks can be built using standard 5e monster creation guidelines.

    Boss-Level Entities¶

    Overlord Veyrix, The Shattered Mind¶

    Large aberration (anomalous psychic), chaotic evil
    Armor Class 18 (psychic barrier)
    Hit Points 210 (20d10+100)
    Speed 30 ft., fly 20 ft. (hover)

    STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
    18 (+4) 14 (+2) 20 (+5) 22 (+6) 18 (+4) 20 (+5)

    Saving Throws Int +11, Wis +9, Cha +10
    Damage Resistances psychic, force; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons
    Condition Immunities charmed, frightened, stunned
    Senses truesight 60 ft., passive Perception 14
    Languages telepathy 120 ft.
    Challenge 14 (11,500 XP)
    Proficiency Bonus +5

    Legendary Resistance (3/day). If Veyrix fails a save, it can choose to succeed instead.

    Psychic Dominion. All creatures of Veyrix’s choice within 60 ft. must succeed on DC 17 Wis save or be charmed and incapacitated until the start of their next turn.

    Actions
    - Mind Rend. Ranged Spell Attack: +11 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (5d10) psychic damage.
    - Mental Collapse (Recharge 5–6). All creatures within 30 ft. make DC 17 Int save or take 36 (8d8) psychic damage and be stunned until the end of their next turn.
    - Ego Shatter. Choose one creature within 60 ft. Target makes DC 17 Wis save or has disadvantage on all attack rolls and saves until end of next turn.

    Legendary Actions (3/round)
    - Psychic Lash. Make a Mind Rend attack.
    - Reality Flicker (Costs 2 Actions). Teleport up to 40 ft. and all enemies within 10 ft. take 14 (4d6) psychic damage.
    - Command Puppet (Costs 3 Actions). Force one charmed creature to make a weapon attack against a target of Veyrix’s choice.
